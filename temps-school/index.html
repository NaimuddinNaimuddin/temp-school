Ramagya Roots

“Children must be taught how to think, not what to think”. – Margaret Mead

Website

Early childhood Education plays a very pivotal role in a toddler’s life; by preparing children emotionally, intellectually, socially and physically to become a world ready citizen.

Pre-school is the first stage in every child’s life when they are separated from the comfort and secure zone of their parents to experience the real world. At Ramagya Roots, we understand this very well and believe in first settling our children into a loving and nurturing environment where children feel at home. As it is going to be their first school experience, our trained teachers take special care to ensure that their school days are full of joyful learning activities which challenge them and develop their love for learning, making the brighter future of our children. With a great legacy of over 15 years, Ramagya Group has been a trendsetter in providing most up-to-date teaching pedagogy,global curriculum, state-of-the-art infrastructure, e-learning and co-curricular activities to ensure holistic growth of children. A dedicated team in Research & Development, highly professional teachers along with quality managers, academic heads and administrators, work towards providing little children the best of education and facilities, to groom them into leaders of tomorrow. Not only this, the school has also taken the onus to give hands-on experience to parents about the learning growth of their children through our Curtain Raiser Activities in each and every Parents’ Teachers Meeting.
